üìä ATTITUDE INFO

Attitude describes how a spacecraft is oriented in 3D space using:

  • Roll: Rotation around optical axis
  • Pitch: Up/down tilt
  • Yaw: Left/right pan

Star trackers determine attitude by matching detected star patterns to known catalogs, providing precise 3D orientation for navigation and pointing.
